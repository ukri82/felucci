There are two types of predictions.

Prior : Predict the score-lines of of the league matches and the qualifiers for the remaining ones BEFORE the world cup starts.
You can change this until 12th June 2014, the start of world cup.

Spot : Just before every match enter the predicted score-lines. There is no predictions for the qualifiers.
You can change this until the start of each match.

Obviously you score more for the prior predictions.
However spot predictions are a great way to improve your scores since you can make better predictions as the world cup advances.


Bets : This is another way to catch up with your friends who are leading.
You don't have to spend any money. Bet with some of your current score each day.
If you get the bet, then you score as many points. Otherwise you lose them :(


Leagues : Normally you are ranked along with all other players.
But you can create leagues and include all your friends in that so that you know where you stand among your friends.
This can be more fun since you compete with only your friends. Your don't have to make different predictions for different leagues.
